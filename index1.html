Tiger

The tiger is the largest cat species, known for its orange coat with black stripes.

Elephant

Elephants are the biggest land animals and are famous for their memory and tusks.

Penguin

Penguins are flightless seabirds that live mostly in the Southern Hemisphere.

Lion

Known as the king of the jungle, lions are powerful big cats that live in groups called prides.
